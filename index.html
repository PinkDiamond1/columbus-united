The project Columbus is a project aiming to facilitate the visualization of the blockchain of ByzCoin.
You can navigate throw the different blocks by going right or left. Then you can have more details about one block by clicking on the wanted block and selecting a transaction. You will see all instructions with their type (spawn, invoke or delete) of each instrucions.
A button is there to browse the whole blockchain in order to find the evolution of this instance throu the blockchain. The evolution will be displayed on the right bottom.
